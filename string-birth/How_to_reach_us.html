The Birth of String Theory 

Florence, May 18 - 19, 2007  

Contrarily to previous announcements, the meeting will be held at The Galileo Galilei Institute for Theoretical Physics, on the hill of Arcetri, 4km south of Florence.

To reach GGI by bus take number 11, in the direction DUE STRADE. Bus stops of interest downtown are located i) in Piazza San Marco; ii) in Via Martelli n.1, at 20 meters from Piazza Duomo); iii) In Piazza Stazione (railway station). Get off at the first stop in VIA DEL GELSOMINO (two stops after PORTA ROMANA), walk your way back till the traffic light and turn right, up the hill, till the top, then turn left and after 100mt cross the main gate and walk up some large stairs till the GGI entrance. Count 15 minutes of bus plus ten minutes walk.

Otherwise follow directions given here.
