The Birth of String Theory 

Florence, May 18 - 19, 2007  

The meeting will be held in two different location:

On Friday, May 18th:
in the "Aula Magna" of Physics Department, in the Scientific Campus of the University of Florence, 8km northwest of Florence.

To get to the department by bus, take number 57, in the direction POLO SCIENTIFICO. Bus stops of interest downtown are located i) in Piazza San Marco; ii) in Via Martelli 1, 20mt from Piazza Duomo); iii) In Piazza Stazione (railway station). Get off at bus stop named SANSONE (polo scientifico itself, in fact; approx. time: 45 min); you will see that most of people get off. You are next to a large red bricks building, which is the Physics Department. The entrance is on the right side.

On Saturday, May 19th:
at The Galileo Galilei Institute for Theoretical Physics, on the hill of Arcetri, 4km south of Florence.

To reach GGI by bus take number 11, in the direction DUE STRADE. Bus stops of interest downtown are located i) in Piazza San Marco; ii) in Via Martelli 1, 20mt from Piazza Duomo); iii) In Piazza Stazione (railway station). Get off at the first stop in VIA DEL GELSOMINO (two stops after PORTA ROMANA), walk your way back till the traffic light and turn right, up the hill, till the top, then turn left and after 100mt cross the main gate and walk up some large stairs till the GGI entrance. Count 15 minutes of bus plus ten minutes walk.

Otherwise follow directions given here.
